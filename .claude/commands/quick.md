# /quick

Answer a quick question in 2-3 sentences max — no fluff, just the key point.

## Description

Use `/quick` when you want a concise, straight-to-the-point answer without explanations, context, or elaboration. Ideal for fast lookups, syntax reminders, or yes/no questions where a short answer is enough.

## Behavior

- Responds in **2–3 sentences maximum**.
- No preamble, no summaries, no filler.
- Just the key point or answer.
